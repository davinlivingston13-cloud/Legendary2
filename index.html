Game Overview

Marvel Legendary is a cooperative deck-building game where players recruit powerful Marvel Super Heroes to defeat malicious Masterminds and foil their catastrophic Schemes. Work together to clear villains out of the city before they escape!

Recruit Heroes

Spend Recruit points to draft powerful heroes into your deck to gain specialized team mechanics and synergies.

Fight Villains

Generate Attack power to defeat villains moving through the city streets and strike directly at the Mastermind.

Stop Schemes

Every scenario features a unique Scheme deck variant that alters the rules and threatens an instant loss if unresolved.
